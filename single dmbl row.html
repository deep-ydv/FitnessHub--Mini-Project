Single Arm Dumbbell Row


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Take one step back into a kunge position. Keep a soft bend in your front leg with the knee in line with your ankle and back leg straight.
  • Lean slightly forward and rest your free hand on dumbbell rack or bench.
  • Lower the dumbbell toward the floor until you have a full extension at the elbow. Maintain proper posture through your shoulders, hip and lower back. Avoid rounding or arching the lumbar spine.
  • Sliding your shoulder blade towards your spine and then lifting the weight up toward your torso by driving your elbow to the ceiling. Keep yor elbows close to your body as it passes the ribs.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Latissimus Dorsi
    ->Terse Major


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Infraspinatus
    -> Posterior Deltoid
    ->Trapezius
    ->Teres Minor
