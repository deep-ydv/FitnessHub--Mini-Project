Smith Machine Incline Bench Press


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(15 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Start by placing an incline bench at a 45 degree angle in the middle of a smith machine, then lie down with your back flat on the bench making sure that the bar is aligned across the upper portion of your chest
  • grab onto the bar with a shoulder - width grip, unlock it and slowly lower it down toward your chest until you feel a stetch in your pectoral muscles.
  • pause, then raise the bar back up without locking your elbows at the top of the movement.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Upper pectoralis major


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Middle & Lower Chest
    -> Anterior Deltoid
    ->Triceps Brachii
