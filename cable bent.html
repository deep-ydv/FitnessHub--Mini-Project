Cable Bent Over Row


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Stand in front of low cable pulley and grasp bar attachment with shoulderwide overhand grip.
  • Step back and bend torso forward until it is about perpendiculaar to cable direction. Keep knees slightly bent while letting arm show towards cable origin.
  • Pull bar attachment towards belly. Let elbows travel close besides torso.
  • Then slowly lower the weight back down to the starting position(look ahead throughtout motion to keep straight. Stabilize body, avoid any swinging).
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Latissimus Dorsi
    ->Terse Major


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Infraspinatus
    -> Posterior Deltoid
    ->Trapezius
    ->Teres Minor
