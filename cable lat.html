Cable Lat Pulldown


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Start by sitting a cable pull down machine that has a wide bar attachment and grab it with a wide overhand grip.
  • Keeping your abs drawn in, back straight and pull down on the bar to your upper chest, keeping your elbows close to your sides.
  • Hold your count at the bottom position, squeeze your lats and then slowly return back to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Latissimus Dorsi


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Infraspinatus
    -> Posterior Deltoid
    ->Trapezius
    ->Teres Minor
    ->Terse Major
