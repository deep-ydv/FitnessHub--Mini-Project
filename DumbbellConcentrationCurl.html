Dumbbell Concentration Curl


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Sit on a bench with feet flat on the floor and hold a dumbbell in one hand, with the elbow resting on the inside of the corresponding thigh.
  • With palm facing upward, curl the weight up toward your shoulder while keeping your upper arm stationary.
  • Pause for a second at the top, then slowly lower the weight back down to starting postion.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Brachialis


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Brachioradialis
    ->Biceps Brachii(short head)
    ->Biceps Brachii(long head)
