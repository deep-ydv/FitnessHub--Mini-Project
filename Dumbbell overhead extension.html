Dumbbell Overhead Extension


BEGINNER 3 SETS(10 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Sit in an upright position and lift the dumbbell to the top of your shoulder. take a deep breath, overlap your hands around the dumbbell, then press it into position averhead.
  • Maintain an overlapping grip and slowly lower the dumbbell behind your head by unlockingyour elbows.
  • Once your forearms reach parallel or just below drive the dumbbellback to the starting point by extendind the elbows and flexing the Triceps.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii


    Secondary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii
