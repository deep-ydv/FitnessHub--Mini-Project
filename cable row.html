Cable Seated Row


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Sit a cable machine with your legs extended and your feet resting on the the floor in front of you and keeping you and keeping your feet slightly bent.
  • Keeping your abs tight and your back Straight, sit in an upright position.
  • slightly lean forward and grab onto the pulleys and pull them towards your chest.
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades togehterat the top. Slowly release the handles back to starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.
