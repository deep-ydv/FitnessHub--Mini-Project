Chest Dip


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(15 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Hold onto parallel bars with your arms straight and feet crossed behind you.
  • Lower yourself down by bending your elbows keeping your body upright and your chest out.
  • Push yourself back up to the starting position while exhaling, squeezing your chest muscles at the top of the movement.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Lower Chest


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Anterior Deltoid
    ->Triceps Brachii
