Incline Dumbbell Fly


BEGINNER 3 SETS(10 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Start by lying on an incline bench set to 45-degree as holding a dumbbell in each hand. left the dumbbell up and over your chest, extending your arms fully as will be your starting position.
  • Slightly retract your shoulder blades, unloack your elbows, and slowly lower the dumbbells laterally while maintaining the angle at your elbow.
  • Once the dumbbell each chest level, reverse the movement by squeezing your pecs together and bringing the dumbbells back to their starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Upper Chest


    Secondary Muscles :

    ->Middle & Lower Chest
    -> Anterior Deltoid
    ->Triceps Brachii
