Seated Machine Fly


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(15 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Sit on the machine with arms fully extended in line with your torse at the level of the middle of your chest.
  • Exhale and bring arms together while keeping them straight.
  • Inhale and bring your arms back to your side, returning to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Inner Chest


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Anterior Deltoid
