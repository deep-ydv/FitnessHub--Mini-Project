Squatting Cable Curl


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • stand facing the cable machine and graps the bar using an underhand grip (palms facing up) with your hand around shoulder width apart.
  • Squat down and rest the back of your arms on ypur knees.
  • Slowly curl the bar up towards your shoulder, isolating the biceps, and hold for a count.
  • Squeeze the biceps, and then slowly lower the weight back to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Biceps Brachii(short head)
    -> Biceps Brachii(long head)


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Brachioradialis
