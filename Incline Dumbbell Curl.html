Inclined Dumbbell Curl


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Position an incline bench at roughly 45 degrees and sit upright with your back flat against the pad. Using a supinated (palms up) grip.
  • Take a deep breath and curl both dumbbells toward your shoulders.
  • Once the biceps fully shortened, slowly lower the weights back to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Biceps Brachii(short head)
    -> Biceps Brachii(long head)


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Brachioradialis
    ->Brachialis
