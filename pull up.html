Pull Up


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Grab the bar with an overhead grip much widder than shoulder-width apart.
  • Exhale and pull yourself up toward the bar by bending your arms, driving your elbows down to your sides, and driving your shoulder blades down and togehter.
  • Inhale and allow yourself to lower your body down until your arms are fully extended, returning to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.

    Primary Muscles :

    -> Latissimus Dorsi


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Brachialis
    -> Teres Major
    ->Biceps Brachii
    ->Brachioradialis
