Seated Ez Barbell Curl


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • start by sitting on the end of a flat bench with your feet flat and your legs spread live a V. Place the barbell on your thighs.
  • Graps a barbell in your hands with a close grip, plams facing up.
  • With your elbow pressed against your inner thigh just above your knees, curl the bar up towards your chest.
  • Squeeze the biceps, and then slowly lower the weight back to the starting postion.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Biceps Brachii(short head)
    ->Biceps Brachii(long head)


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Brachioradialis
