Barbell Bench Press


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Lie on a bench with a barbell positioned above your chest.
  • Grasp the bar with an overhand grip, hand shoulder-width apart.
  • Lower the bar towards your chest, keeping your elbows close to your sides.
  • Push the bar back up to starting postion squeezing your chest muscles at the top.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Upper Chest
    -> Middle Chest


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Lower Chest
    -> Anterior Deltoid
    ->Triceps Brachii
