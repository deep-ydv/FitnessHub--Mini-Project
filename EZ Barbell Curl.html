Ez Barbell Curl


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Standing up straight with your feet shoulder width apart, knees slightly bent and abs drawn in.
  • Grab an Ez bar with hands closer than shoulder width apart and palms facing upward.
  • keeping your elbows fixed to your sides, raise the bar towards yoyr chest, squeezing your biceps as much as possible.
  • Pause for moment and then return to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Biceps Brachii(short head)
    ->Biceps Brachii(long head)


    Secondary Muscles :

    -> Brachioradialis
    ->Brachialis
