Cable Straight Arm Pulldown


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Stand facing a cable machine with a rope attachment. Grasp the handle with a pronated grip (double overhead) at roughly shoulder width and lean forward slightly by hinging at the hips.
  • Keeping your wrist steady and a slight bend in your elbow, pull the bar down until it touches your upper thighs.
  • Feeling a stretch in your lats and tricep muscles, keeping your elbows closw to your sides.
  • Slowly release the rope back up to starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Lattissimus Dorsi


    Secondary Muscles :
