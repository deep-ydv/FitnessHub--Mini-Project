Tricep Dumbbell Kickback


BEGINNER 3 SETS(10 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Hold a dumbbell in your right hand and place your left knee and hand on a bench, keeping your back straight.
  • Bring the weight up to your chest, with your elbow at a 90-degree angle.
  • Extend your right arm behind you ,keeping your elbow close to your body.
  • Slowly lower the weight back down to the starting position and repeat on the other.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps..


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii


    Secondary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii
