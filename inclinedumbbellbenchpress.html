Incline Dumbbell Bench Press


BEGINNER 3 SETS(12 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Lie on a incline bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward.
  • Lower the weight your chest, keeping your elbows close to your side.
  • Push the weight back up to starting position squeezing your chest muscles at the top.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Upper Chest


    Secondary Muscles :

    ->Middle & Lower Chest
    -> Anterior Deltoid
    ->Triceps Brachii
