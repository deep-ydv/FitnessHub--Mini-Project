Standing EZ Bar FrenchPress


BEGINNER 3 SETS(10 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Stand up straight with your feet around shoulder width apart and a slight bend in your kness. lift the Ez bar above your head and bend at your elbows slightly to take the tension onto your tricpes. your paims are now upward.
  • Slowly lower the bar behind your head , bending at the elbows, so that it goes down in a back.squeeze the triceps as you lower the bar down and then hold for a count.
  • Then slowly raise the bar back to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps..

    Primary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii
