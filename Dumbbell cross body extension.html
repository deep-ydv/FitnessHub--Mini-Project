Dumbbell Cross Body Extension


BEGINNER 3 SETS(10 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Lay down on the bench and hold the dumbbell straight up in the air with your right arm.
  • With your elbow pointed outwards bend at the elbow and lower the dumbbell across your body to your left shoulder.
  • Bring the dumbbell back up and lock out your arm again. this completes one repetition.
  • Repeat for the desired number of repetition and then switch arms.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii


    Secondary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii
