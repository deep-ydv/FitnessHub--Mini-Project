Cable Rope Pushdown


BEGINNER 3 SETS(10 REPS)
INTERMEDIATE 4 SETS(12 REPS)
REST 60 SECONDS

Instructions :


  • Start off standing in front of a cable machine attaching a rope to the high pilley and grabbing the attachment with an overhand..
  • Keeping your abs drawn in, back straight and elbows an at your side, as you push down towards your thighs, split the rope apart at the bottom and isolate the tricep muscle.
  • Slowly lower under control back to the starting position.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps.


    • Targeted Muscles :



    Primary Muscles :

    -> Triceps Brachii


    Secondary Muscles :

    ->Triceps Brachii
